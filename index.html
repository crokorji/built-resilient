// Who I Am
I COACH WHAT I LIVE

I'm Clifford — a health and performance coach with over a decade in the trenches. I'm also a competitive powerlifter, a dad to a toddler, and someone who believes your health comes before your ego.

I use tools like PNOE metabolic testing and InBody composition analysis to take the guesswork out of programming. Every programme I write is backed by data, built on evidence, and designed for your life — not someone else's highlight reel.

I've worked with office workers who can't touch their toes, powerlifters chasing national records, climbers building finger strength, and endurance athletes hunting PBs. The common thread? Health first. Always.

Philosophy
Health is the baseline. Performance is built on top. Never the other way around.
Method
Data-driven programming. Metabolic testing, movement screening, and honest conversation — then we build the plan.
Standard
I compete, I train, I get it wrong, I learn. If I'm asking you to do it, I've done it myself.
